About Amit

Capturing emotions in
random moments, perfectly

I'm Amit — better known as randomclicker.exe. I specialize in finding beauty in the unscripted. My work is about capturing raw emotions as they happen, often through dramatic silhouettes and cinematic street scenes.

Based in India. Whether I'm exploring the ghats of Varanasi or chasing light in urban alleys, I'm always looking for that one frame that tells a story. And yes, I'm known to smile like a quokka when I nail the perfect shot. 😊
