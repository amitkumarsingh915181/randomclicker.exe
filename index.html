The Photographer

Capturing raw truth in fleeting seconds

I am Amit — better known across the digital realm as randomclicker.exe. My creative philosophy centres on finding profound visual elegance in unscripted accidents. Whether documenting the timeless mysticism of Varanasi ghats or navigating the sunlit geometry of urban alleys, I search for moments where dramatic light meets human emotion.

My work bridges raw documentary street life with polished editorial composition. Every shutter click is an exploration of patience, instinct, and optical storytelling.
